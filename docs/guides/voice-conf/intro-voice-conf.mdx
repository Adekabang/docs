Dyte's voice conferencing platform, built upon [WebRTC](https://dyte.io/blog/webrtc/), enables you to seamlessly incorporate real-time communication features into your web and mobile applications. Using Dyte's REST APIs and client-side SDKs, you can effortlessly acquire, transmit, and play media while efficiently managing network connections within your applications. Dyte's voice conferencing comes with in-built noise cancellation (powered by Krisp).

- [Essentials](#essentials): Step by step guides to help you through the setup process and fundamental concepts
- [Capabilities](#capabilities): Guides to functionalities that you can incrementally add to enhance your voice conferencing experience
- [UI Kit](#ui-kit): Learn about Dyte's prebuilt library of UI components through guides on integrating them and customizing them to align with your brand's identity
- [Reference](#reference): Build a better understanding of the core SDK that provides APIs driving your live streaming experience along with references for the REST APIs that power your voice conferences
